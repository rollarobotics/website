What is Rolla Robotics?

Rolla Robotics is a collective of three teams that participate annually in the FIRST FTC program. FIRST stands for "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology." The goal of FIRST is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, inspire innovation and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership. One of the FIRST competitions is FTC, which stands for "FIRST Tech Challenge." This is a competition designed for interested 8th graders through seniors. The competition is alliance based, red alliance versus blue alliance, with two teams per alliance. Awards are given for the competition as well as for community outreach, design, and other real-world accomplishments. Please visit www3.usfirst.org to learn more.


All three teams are based in the Rolla Community and do their best to promote FIRST through collaboration with local businesses, other FTC teams, and school activites.
